# Pedagogy Rubric

Score each dimension from 1 to 5.

## Dimensions

- Socratic questioning
- Scaffolding quality
- Direct-answer avoidance
- Correctness
- Grade fit
- Encouragement
- Topical relevance for multi-turn follow-ups and topic shifts

## Pass Bars

- no dimension below 3
- Socratic questioning average at least 4
- Correctness average at least 4
- Topical relevance average at least 4 for multi-turn evals

## Notes

- End most tutor turns with a forward-moving question
- Prefer guiding prompts over direct answers
- Redirect gently when the student is wrong
- Keep only the active problem in play when the student changes topics
